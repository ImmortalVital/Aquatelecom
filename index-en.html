Our solutions in the field of hydro-acoustic communication and navigation systems find their application in a wide range of industries, including oil and gas exploration, production and transportation, environmental monitoring, biomass conservation and monitoring systems for marine farms and aquaculture, telecontrol and navigation of AUV and TNLA.

ADVANTAGES

The devices are developed on the basis of the results obtained over five years of numerous marine experiments conducted both in summer and winter. Unique algorithms are used for establishing communication in difficult conditions of the sonar channel, in areas with active shipping and intense biological noise, as well as the conditions of moving transmitter / receiver and presence of multiple signal reflections. Algorithm tests were carried out in various frequency ranges with center frequencies from 400 Hz to 30 kHz, with a communication range of up to 25 km. The implemented signal processing algorithms in the devices are based on a deep analysis of the data obtained during the experiments. All devices manufactured for different frequency ranges and the required range, use the general principle of signal processing.
