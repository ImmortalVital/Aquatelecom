Hidroacoustic mode version 1.0

Specification:

Receiver frequency band: 24-32, 10-14 kHz
Voltage: 12,5 - 24 V
Power consumption (reception / transmission), W: 2,4/max 45 W
Connection Interface: Ethernet / RS232

Key experiments conducted with the developed equipment in the range of 24-32 kHz:
• Error in measuring the propagation time of the signal in the asynchronous mode: 20 μs (± 3 cm at a distance of 500 m with SZ = 1500 m / s.The experiment was conducted in the winter of 2017 on the ice surface of the sea using DGPS)
• Information traffic speed (confirmed by sea tests in winter and fall): up to 1 kbit / s at a distance of 3 km (maximum modem bandwidth: up to 4 kbit / s). Error probability (confirmed by sea trials in 2017-2019): 10^-5
Providing HA communications and navigation in a network of several beacons at a relative speed of nodes up to 1.5 m / s in the conditions of noise of ANVA engines. The maximum positioning error of moving objects was less than 2 m (most of the oscillations were made by the DGPS antenna on the ANVA during sea waves during tests in the shallow Patroclus Bay with complex hydrology at a distance of 1 km in 2018)
